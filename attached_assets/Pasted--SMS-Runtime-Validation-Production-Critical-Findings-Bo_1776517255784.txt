**SMS Runtime Validation — Production (Critical Findings)**

**Bottom line:** Twilio is fully failing in production (401), and the majority of outbound SMS paths silently drop messages without logging, persistence, or event emission. This is a system integrity issue, not just a credential issue.

---

### 1) Twilio Status — **Confirmed Broken**
All tested credentials (master + sub-account) return:

- `401 Unauthorized`
- `Error 20003 (Authenticate)`

**Impact:**
- 100% of outbound SMS attempts fail

---

### 2) Runtime Behavior — **Inconsistent and Unsafe**

6 outbound SMS call sites audited:

- 2 → surface failure to caller
- 4 → **silent failure (console.error only)**

**Silent paths include:**
- Crash alerts to owners
- Hot lead notifications
- Push fallback SMS

These are high-priority business-critical messages.

---

### 3) Observability Gap — **Severe**

Across all SMS flows:

- ❌ No retry logic
- ❌ No `message.failed` event emission
- ❌ No integration health updates per failure
- ❌ No consistent user-visible failure surface

---

### 4) Database Integrity — **Broken**

- `messages.error_message` = NULL for all rows
- Failed sends often do not create a row at all

**Result:**
- No audit trail
- No failure analytics
- No post-incident debugging capability

---

### 5) System Mismatch

- Integration Health → **accurately reports Twilio failure**
- Runtime Messaging → **silently drops messages**

This creates a false sense of operational reliability.

---

### Root Issue

Outbound messaging lacks an enforced failure contract.  
Each call site handles failure differently, resulting in inconsistent and often silent behavior.

---

### Required Actions (in order)

1. **Fix Twilio credentials**
   - Update `TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN`
   - Verify via integration health turning green

2. **Implement centralized SMS send wrapper**
   - Enforce:
     - message row write (success + failure)
     - `error_message` population
     - `message.failed` event emission
   - Remove per-route handling differences

3. **Eliminate silent failure paths**
   - Replace all `console.error`-only catches
   - No SMS failure should be unobserved

---

### Risk Summary

If credentials were rotated without updating production:

- All owner alerts, lead notifications, and fallback SMS have been failing
- A significant portion were silently dropped
- There is currently no reliable way to quantify impact

---

### Unverified

- Origin of existing `failed` message row (id=207)
- Whether `enforceSmsProvider()` introduces additional silent short-circuiting

---

**Conclusion:**  
Phase B successfully exposed the failure.  
The messaging layer must now be upgraded to match that level of honesty and observability.
