That is a **predatory growth strategy** in the best way possible. It’s exactly how software giants like ClickFunnels or GHL captured the market early—by making the deal so "stupid" that people feel like they’re losing money by not signing up.

By combining a **30-Day Blitz** with **Grandfathered Pricing**, you aren't just selling software; you're selling a "Legacy Asset." Users will stay subscribed for years just because they don't want to lose that 50% rate.

### 🔱 The "Blitz" Strategy Breakdown

#### Phase 1: The 30-Day Blitz (The Land Grab)

* **The Deal:** 50% off all tiers ($48.50, $148.50, $248.50).
* **The Hook:** "Grandfathered for Life." As long as the sub stays active, the price never goes up.
* **The Urgency:** Use a "Global Counter" on the landing page showing the 30-day clock.
* **Goal:** Capture 50–100 high-leverage users who will act as your "Case Studies."

#### Phase 2: The Founders Deal (The Stabilization)

* **The Deal:** Full Price, but 60 Days Free.
* **The Hook:** "We've closed the Blitz, but we're giving you 2 months to get profitable before we bill you."
* **Goal:** Scale the MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) back to healthy margins while still offering a "low-risk" entry.

### 🔱 My Opinion: Why this works for you

1. **Viral Potential:** Influencers and Agency owners love "Grandfather" deals. They will promote you for free just to tell their audience, "Get in now before the price doubles."
2. **Churn Killer:** Your churn rate (people quitting) will be near zero. If they quit, they lose the 50% discount forever. That "loss aversion" keeps your bank account stable.
3. **Liquidity:** The 50% off deal gets you cash *now* to pay for your Replit Core, Vapi, and OpenAI costs while you're still growing.
