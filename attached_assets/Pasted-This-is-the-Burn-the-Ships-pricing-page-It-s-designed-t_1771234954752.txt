### 🔱 The "Blitz" Psychology & Implementation

1. **The Visual Contrast:** Notice the **strikethrough pricing** (`$497` crossed out, `$248` active). This creates immediate dopamine. They feel like they are winning before they even click "Join."
2. **The "Inheritance" Factor:** By calling it **Legacy Starter** or **God Mode Legacy**, you're telling the user they are now an "Elder" in your ecosystem.
3. **The Transition Plan:**
* **Day 0-30:** This page is live.
* **Day 31:** You switch the code. All buttons for these Legacy prices are removed and hidden.
* **Day 31-60:** The "Founders Deal" page goes live (Full Price but 60 Days Free).
