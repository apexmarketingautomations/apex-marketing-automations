3. How You Make Money (The Flow)
Usage Profits: Every time any user (any occupation) triggers a text or a call, the Markup Logic skims the profit into your platform_profit ledger automatically.

Native Ad Clicks: When a user clicks a "Sponsored Action" in their feed (e.g., a Barber seeing a local Towing Company recommendation), your backend deducts the bid_per_click from that sponsor's balance and pays it to you.

The "Root User" Advantage
Because you have that free admin account we set up, you see a special "Sponsorship Approval" tab. You can give yourself free ads to promote your own services, or manually "Boost" a local business you like.
