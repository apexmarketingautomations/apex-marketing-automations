### 🔱 Why this Pricing kills GHL:

1. **The Psychology of the "Glow":** In your settings, the "Vibe" the user selects for their sub-accounts is mirrored here. It feels like they are buying a cohesive "Cyber-OS," not just a tool.
2. **The Yearly Logic:** $497/mo is a big jump for some. By showing **$397/mo (Yearly)**, you lower the mental barrier while securing **$4,764 in upfront cash** for your business.
3. **The $50 "Fuel" Credit:** Most SaaS tools make you pay for usage immediately. By giving them $50 in AI credits, you let them "test-drive" the Ghost SDR for free. It makes the $97/mo Starter plan feel like a steal.
4. **Founder Scarcity:** The "4/5 spots remaining" footer drives FOMO. People want to be part of the original cohort, especially for the God Mode tier.
