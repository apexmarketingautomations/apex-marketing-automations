```sql
SELECT 
  id, 
  report_number,
  data->'officialFlhsmv'->'detail'->'Vehicles' as vehicle_data
FROM crash_reports
WHERE status = 'COMPLETED' 
  AND data ? 'officialFlhsmv';
```

**Operational Notes for your retrieval:**
1. **Status Check:** If you don't see the data, ensure the report status is `COMPLETED`. If it is `AWAITING` or `PENDING`, the worker hasn't finished the FLHSMV handshake yet.

3. **Data Integrity:** The `crashReportWorker` is designed to be atomic. If the data isn't there, the `officialFlhsmv` key will be null.
