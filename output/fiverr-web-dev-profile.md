## PROFILE OPTIMIZATION TIPS

### Profile Photo
- Use a professional, high-quality headshot with good lighting
- Smile naturally — you want to appear approachable and trustworthy
- Use a clean, simple background (solid color or blurred)
- Avoid sunglasses, heavy filters, or group photos
- 400x400 pixels minimum; Fiverr crops to a circle

### Response Time
- Aim to respond to messages within 1 hour during business hours
- Set up the Fiverr mobile app for push notifications
- Use Quick Responses for common questions
- Fiverr rewards fast responders with better visibility in search

### Getting Your First Reviews
- Start with competitive pricing to attract initial buyers
- Deliver exceptional quality on every order — early reviews shape your reputation
- Fiverr automatically prompts buyers to leave reviews after delivery
- Over-deliver on value to naturally earn positive feedback

### Gig Optimization
- Use all 5 gig images — include mockups, screenshots, and "what you'll get" graphics
- Create a short gig video (even a simple screen recording with voiceover works well)
- Use relevant search tags — research what buyers are searching for
- Write gig descriptions that focus on buyer benefits, not just your skills
- Update gigs regularly to stay relevant in Fiverr's algorithm

### Seller Levels & Rankings
- **New Seller**: Complete your profile 100%, get your first few orders
- **Level 1**: 10 orders + 60 days + 4.7+ rating — unlock promoted gigs
- **Level 2**: 50 orders + 120 days + 4.7+ rating — higher visibility, more features
- **Top Rated**: By invitation — Fiverr's highest tier for consistent performers

### General Tips
- Complete your Fiverr profile 100% — education, certifications, linked accounts
- Add a profile description video (2 minutes max)
- Join Fiverr's Seller Plus program when available for advanced analytics
- Be active daily — Fiverr favors active sellers in search rankings
- Never ask for reviews in exchange for discounts (against Fiverr's ToS)
- Specialize your gigs — niche expertise outperforms "I do everything" profiles
- Use Buyer Requests to find new clients proactively
